U.M.P.I.R.E.

Unidentified and Missing People Investigation, Registry, and Education

We specialize in finding and naming people from marginalized communities. People who otherwise get ignored, devalued, diminished, silenced.

We liaise with law enforcement on your behalf, so you don't have to.

We never give up. We keep looking. No case is too hard.

Our methods include:

  • Forensic Genetic Geneaology: family trees based on DNA testing,
  • Web sleuthing: searching social media, news paper archives, and public databases world-wide,
  • Building solid report with agencies and research centers across the globe.

Our database is protected. We accept entries from the public in general, but give read access only to trusted contacts, and law enforcement if so required.
